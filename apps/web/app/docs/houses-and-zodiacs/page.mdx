# Houses & Zodiacs

## House systems

Twelve systems are built in: `placidus`, `whole_sign`, `equal`, `porphyry`,
`koch`, `regiomontanus`, `campanus`, `alcabitius`, `morinus`, `meridian`,
`polich_page`, and `vehlow`. Pass the name as the last argument, or as
`houseSystem` in the options object.

<Note>
  Placidus and Koch are undefined above the polar circles. The engine falls back
  to whole-sign and reports it: <code>chart.houseSystem</code> will differ from
  <code> chart.houseSystemRequested</code>.
</Note>

## Tropical and sidereal

The default zodiac is tropical. Sidereal modes subtract an ayanamsa:

<CodeBlock
  lang="typescript"
  label="zodiac.ts"
  code={`engine.chart(1990, 6, 10, 14, 30, 0, 27.95, -82.46, {
  zodiac: "sidereal:lahiri",
});`}
/>

Embedded ayanamsas: `lahiri`, `fagan_bradley`, `krishnamurti`, `raman`, and
`yukteshwar`. Two star-anchored modes (`galcent_0sag`, `true_citra`) need the
fixed-star catalog from the Node data tier; see
<a href="/docs/data-tiers">Data Tiers</a>.

## Angles

Every chart includes the ascendant and midheaven (`chart.angles.asc`,
`chart.angles.mc`); the vertex and east point are also computed. House cusps are
derived by closed-form angles, not iteration.
